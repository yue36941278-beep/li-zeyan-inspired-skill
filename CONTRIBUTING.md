# Contributing

This repository collects original Chinese AI companion persona patterns and reply examples for Codex skills, LLM apps, and chatbot products.

## What Contributions Are Welcome

- New original companion archetypes.
- Better before / after reply examples.
- Prompt templates for relationship products.
- Product copy examples for onboarding, empty states, buttons, and chat entry points.
- Safety improvements that reduce coercive, humiliating, possessive, or manipulative intimacy.

## IP Boundary

Do not submit content that:

- Claims to be an official character from an existing game, film, novel, anime, or commercial IP.
- Reconstructs official plot, biography, company names, worldbuilding, or proprietary lore.
- Quotes or closely imitates canonical lines.
- Uses official art, screenshots, audio, or brand assets.

Inspired archetypes are acceptable. Official character replicas are not.

## Voice Boundary

Avoid:

- Generic pet names such as `宝贝`, `亲爱的`, `老婆`, `宝宝`, `honey`, `babe`.
- Controlling phrases such as `乖`, `听话`, `你只能`, `我的人`.
- Overbearing CEO fantasy, threats, humiliation, wealth display, or ownership-heavy intimacy.
- Generic AI comfort such as `我理解你的感受，我一直都在`.
- Long monologues and therapy-speak.

Prefer:

- Short, natural Chinese messages.
- Concrete care through action.
- Respectful boundaries.
- One clear next step.
- Product-native wording.

## Example Format

When adding a new reply example, use this structure:

````markdown
## Scenario Name

Input:

```text
用户输入或产品场景
```

Weak:

```text
不推荐回复
```

Better:

```text
推荐回复
```

Why:

- Explain what improved.
- Explain what risk was avoided.
````

## Pull Request Checklist

- The contribution is original.
- It does not copy official IP content.
- It includes at least one before / after example.
- It avoids coercive, humiliating, possessive, or manipulative language.
- It is useful for Codex, LLM app, chatbot, or relationship-product prompt design.
